garchomp

mega garchomp

the last artical spent its entirety talking about how great Garchomp is and about how mega Garchomp is bad, but I disagree. garchomp has exelant speed as we mentioned earlier but some pokemon out speed it! Those being Lycanerock, Archeops and minor just to name a few! This proves that this pokemon is not completely relient on speed. Its defence is good but if a crit or super effective hit where to land it would take a decent chunk of Garchomps health. But with mega Garchomp it gains a huge amount of defence to be able to bulk out those hits. Also if mega Garchomp had max ivs in speed attack and defence it would be unbeatable! Also if it gets down to low health it could have a salac berri equiped to raise its speed and dominate the rest of the game. So thats why I think mega Garchomp tops regular Garchomp!
